About CivicEase

CivicEase is a citizen-centric platform designed to simplify the process of reporting and tracking civic issues. We bridge the gap between the public and municipal departments through a seamless, transparent, and digital grievance redressal system.

Our Mission

To empower citizens with a voice and provide local authorities with the tools to efficiently resolve civic complaints, promoting cleaner, safer, and smarter communities.

Key Features

  • User-friendly complaint submission
  • Real-time tracking with unique complaint codes
  • Department-side dashboard with filtering
  • Status updates via smart checkboxes
  • Serverless and scalable architecture

Meet the Team

We are a team of developers, designers, and civic-tech enthusiasts participating in HackVortex 2024. Our goal is to make civic governance more efficient and people-focused using smart, low-cost technology solutions.
